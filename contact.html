How often are results updated?

Results are updated as soon as they are officially announced by the Nagaland State Lottery authorities, typically within minutes of the draw.

Are the results on this website official?

We display results from official sources, but we recommend verifying with the official Nagaland State Lottery department before claiming prizes.

Can I download result images?

Yes! You can download high-quality images of the lottery results for your records. Just click the download button on any result.

Do you sell lottery tickets?

No, we do not sell lottery tickets. We are an information service that provides lottery results only. Please purchase tickets from authorized dealers.

Is this service free?

Yes, all our services are completely free. You can check results, download images, and access historical data at no cost.

How can I report incorrect results?

If you notice any discrepancies, please contact us immediately using the form above. We take accuracy very seriously.
